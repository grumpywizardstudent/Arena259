# Creature Proposal
Sebastian Porto

## Name
The Abominable MudMan

## Health
120

## Attack Description
When MudMan attacks, it turns 5% of the opponents blood into mud, and deals a consistent 9 damage per attack. each turn after the MudMan attacks, there is a chance (equal to % mud in opponent, up to 40%) for the mud in the opponents blood to migrate and cause an aneurism, dealing 10% of the creatures remaining health as damage

## Special Ability (optional)
Each fire based attack inflicted on MudMan raises his defense by 4% (max of 20%), but lowers his health by 3% (max 36 health loss)

Each water based attack inflicted on Mudman lowers his defense by 3% (max of 15%), but raises his health by 4% (max 24 health gain)

## Weakness
high health, but low damage and relies on many subsoquent attacks to reliably proc its special ability.
