## Name: Ooze
## Health: 120
## Attack Description
Each ooze deals ((18/num_oozes)+1) damage every turn.

## Special Ability (optional)
Whenever an ooze takes damage, it divides itself in two, splitting all attributes in half (rounded down).

## Weakness
Relatively low, but consistent damage.
